At the **ZidaLab**, we pioneer **microfluidic** technologies for **single-molecule and single-cell analysis** with the mission of driving innovation in **precision medicine**.

Our research lies at the intersection of biotechnology, micro/nano-engineering, and computational analysis ‚Äî developing diagnostic tools that are **smarter, more sensitive, and more accessible**.

---

## üîç Our Vision

We are transforming the way diseases are diagnosed by:

- Designing **label-free digital nucleic acid tests** for cost-effective genetic diagnostics  
- Innovating high-throughput tools for **single-cell transcriptomics**  
- Harnessing **deep learning** and image-based analysis to unlock the potential of droplet-based assays  
- Redefining the role of microfluidics in real-world clinical and biomedical applications

[**Explore Our Research ‚Üí**](research)

## üöÄ Spotlight Technologies

- **Deep-dLAMP**: A digital nucleic acid test that uses droplet volume statistics and precipitate-based detection ‚Äî no fluorescence required. 
- **StratoLAMP**: The world‚Äôs first label-free multiplex droplet assay, distinguishing gene targets by precipitate morphology using deep learning  
- **CP-seq**: A breakthrough single-cell screening platform enabling combinatorial perturbation analysis with unmatched throughput and efficiency  

These platforms challenge the conventional boundaries of genetic testing and single-cell analysis ‚Äî making advanced diagnostics more scalable and affordable.
